Spot vs Futures:
• SPOT: Index spot data (e.g., NIFTY 50 index)
• FUTURES: Derivative futures data (trading instrument)

Accepted formats:
- Column names are case-insensitive (Open, open, OPEN all work)
- Date: YYYY-MM-DD or DD-MM-YYYY
- Time: HH:MM:SS (optional)
- If date is missing, sequential dates will be assigned

Example: date, time, open, high, low, close, volume
